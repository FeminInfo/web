Why FeminInfo?

When deciding what we wish existed in the world, we decided an accessible women's health app was just that. We wanted to apply our knowledge of JavaScript, HTML, and CSS to more complex programming, and in doing so created a mobile website. We hope you enjoy! Email us with feedback or questions at femininfotracker@gmail.com
